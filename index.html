04. white noise and honey on skin

white noise and honey on skin

Computer generated poetry zine, 2018, 8 pages


white noise and honey on skin is a juxtaposition of the script of the 2000 film American Psycho and the romantic prose from the Diary of Anais Nin. These distinctly different texts were analyzed through an input system, which generated phrases following every appearance of the eponymous words. The resulting descriptions collaged together creates an imagery of a vague unsettling feeling: a buzzing uncertainty, something brutal and romantic all at once.

01. chimera

chimera

Issue 01: Fetish, 2019, 68 pages, 7" x 9.5"


chimera is a collection of essays, interviews and film photographs dedicated to disrupting race-based fantasies with authenticity. The premiere issue of the publication focuses on the perception of East Asian women, as an examination of personal experiences with fetishization. Issue 01: Fetish aims to illuminate the danger of gendered stereotyping, while embracing Asian femininity as a form of empowerment.

The online platform is an archive containing photographs from each issue, as well as photos of external cultural context. It is open to public submissions: chimera
